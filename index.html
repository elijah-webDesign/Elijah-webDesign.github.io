Please Excuse my Lack of Creativity

The grass is green.

The sky is blue.

Heavenly God, why must you burn us with the light of you?

This semester has been long, even though it's been a week.

Surely college cannot be handled by the weak.

We all will prevail, but we still act like snails.

No worry!

Just give us a second!

For we are Academic Weapons.
